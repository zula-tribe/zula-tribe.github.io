About Zula

Zula is on a mission to enhance diversity and inclusion in children's libraries and bookshelves worldwide! Our goal is to create an inclusive online library for children's books, offering both open-source and premium subscription options. In this library, we curate a wide range of books that represent diverse cultures, backgrounds, and experiences. Our aim is to provide children of all backgrounds with access to high-quality literature that reflects their identities and promotes understanding and empathy.


By offering an open-source platform, we encourage collaboration and contribution from authors, illustrators, and creators who share our vision. Together, we can build a rich collection of inclusive children's books that inspire, educate, and entertain young readers. In addition to the open-source content, we also offer a premium subscription service. Subscribers gain access to exclusive titles, enhanced features, and personalized recommendations, providing an enriched reading experience for children and their families.


Join us on this exciting journey as we work towards creating a more inclusive world, one book at a time!

Our Mission

Zula's Mission is to champion diversity and inclusion in children's literature by creating an expansive online library that offers both open-source and premium subscription options, ensuring that children from all walks of life have access to inclusive and empathetic content.

Our Goals

Curate Diverse and Inclusive Library

Curate an expansive collection of children's books from a wide array of cultures, backgrounds, and experiences. Ensure that the library represents diverse identities and promotes understanding, empathy, and acceptance among young readers.

Foster Collaborative Creation

Establish an open-source platform that invites authors, illustrators, and creators who share our vision to contribute and collaborate. Encourage the growth of a vibrant community-driven ecosystem, resulting in a rich repository of inclusive children's literature.

Enhance User Access and Engagement

Develop user-friendly apps and interfaces to facilitate seamless access to the diverse content within the library. Prioritize the dissemination of diverse literature to reach children and families globally, fostering a love for reading and promoting cultural appreciation.

Deliver Premium Enrichment

Introduce a premium subscription service that offers exclusive titles, advanced features, and personalized recommendations. Provide subscribers with an enriched reading experience that supports their journey of discovery, learning, and celebration of differences.

Our Vision

Through Zula's commitment to enhancing diversity and inclusion in children's libraries, we strive to cultivate a generation of young minds that embrace differences, celebrate diversity, and build a more inclusive world through the power of literature.

Our Values

Our values are the guiding principles that drive every decision we make at Zula. They reflect who we are and what we stand for. Our desire with the project is to bring exciting, funny, entertaining, and empowering stories to as many children as possible. Families who cannot necessarily afford books should also have access to our books. This is achieved by making the texts available and free.:


  • Inclusivity: We believe that every child deserves to see themselves in the stories they read. Inclusivity is at the core of our mission, and we strive to make our library a welcoming space for all.

  • Empathy: We promote understanding and empathy through the power of literature. Our books encourage readers to step into the shoes of others and embrace diverse perspectives.

  • Collaboration: Collaboration is key to our success. We value the contributions of our community, and together, we create a rich tapestry of diverse children's literature.

  • Quality: We are dedicated to providing high-quality content that educates, entertains, and inspires. Our commitment to excellence is unwavering.

  • Growth: We believe in continuous growth and improvement, both as individuals and as a platform. We adapt, learn, and evolve to better serve our users and our mission.
